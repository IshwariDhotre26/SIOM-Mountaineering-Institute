-------- TRAINING AREAS ---------

Adventure Courses :-

At SIOM, under the guidance of Giripremi’s Everesters, some of the best rock climbers and experienced educators; participants undergo scientific and methodical training for adventure courses. With the motto of ‘Adventure for All‘ and with paramount focus on safety, SIOM aims at inculcating the spirit of adventure among every section of society.The aim of these long trekking programme is to inculcate the spirit of adventure, make the new generation bold and confident, and give them memorable experiences, they shall cherish forever.

Mountaineering Expeditions :-

Being a mountaineering institute, SIOM organizes guided expedition for novice as well as experienced mountaineers. Apart from these expeditions, we organize customized expedition as per the requirement of the group.For this expeditions we will trained the members under the Everesters.One year training program will be held for them which includes acclimatization also.

Tour De Sahyadri :-

Western Ghats are one of the natural wonders of India and the area is one of the world’s ten Hottest biodiversity hotspots with home to flowering plants, mammal, amphibian, reptiles, insects and bird species. The ghat offers a stunning Landscape and Natural Scenic Places.In this Tour De Sahyadri programme, we also add some elements of Adventure programme as you explore through the remote sections of Hills and valleys.
